The Yggdrasil Core — A Unified Platform

The foundation of the ecosystem is the "Yggdrasil Core," a standardized architecture binding all devices. This unified approach is the key to scalability, interoperability, and manufacturing efficiency.

The Core's Weave: System Architecture

We employ a custom System-in-Package (SiP) for core wearables like the Seidr Ring and Skuld's Mark, achieving extreme functional density in a ~23 mm² footprint. For larger devices like the Valkyrie Scout drone, a conventional rigid-flex PCB maintains firmware portability across the entire product line.

  • Primary SoC: Nordic nRF54L Series
  • UWB Transceiver: For spatial awareness
  • PMIC: Intelligent Power Management IC
  • eSE: Embedded Secure Element for warding data

Whispers on the Wind: Hybrid Wireless

Our tri-protocol strategy ensures flawless communication:

  • BLE Mesh: The ambient, always-on fabric for device discovery and quiet commands.
  • NFC: For intuitive, secure, point-to-point binding rituals like payments and pairing.
  • UWB: The key to "spell-casting." Centimeter-level, low-latency (<5ms) spatial data for gesture recognition and secure access.

The Unbroken Circle: Perpetual Power

A multi-faceted energy harvesting system creates near-perpetual operation. An intelligent PMIC (Nordic nPM1300) blends power from multiple inconsistent sources.

  • Thermoelectric Generators (TEGs): Converts body heat into power on devices like Freya's Torc.
  • Kinetic Energy Harvesters (KEH): Converts motion into electricity, ideal for the Draupnir Band.
  • Ambient RF Harvesting: A constant micro-watt trickle charge, offsetting battery self-discharge.

Wards & Wisdom: Embedded Intelligence

A privacy-first architecture is paramount.

  • On-Device AI: TensorFlow Lite for Microcontrollers runs gesture models directly on the SoC for instantaneous response.
  • Federated Learning: Personalization occurs on-device; only anonymized model updates are sent to the cloud, protecting raw user data.
  • Transaction-Grade Security: A hardware-based embedded Secure Element (eSE) creates a tamper-proof vault for tokens and credentials.

The Path of Creation: A Phased Forging

Our development is a three-tiered saga, de-risking the project by conquering foundational challenges first.

Tier 1 — The Bindings of Midgard (Seidr Ring, Skuld's Mark)

Challenge: Extreme miniaturization and biocompatibility.

Solution: Multi-layer rigid-flex PCBs with rolled-annealed copper for the ring. For the temporary tattoo, conductive graphene inks are printed on a biocompatible substrate, creating an epidermal electronic system.

Tier 2 — Eyes of Asgard (Odin's Sight, Valkyrie Scout)

Challenge: Advanced optics and low-observability robotics.

Solution: For the spectacles, we start with current-gen waveguide displays while pursuing R&D in next-gen micro-LED retinal projection. For the drone, ultra-quiet propulsion systems (<35 dBA) and active camouflage using e-ink panels are engineered for stealth.

Tier 3 — Connecting the Realms (The Nine Realms Network)

Challenge: Scalable, ultra-low-latency cloud infrastructure.

Solution: WebRTC (Web Real-Time Communication) to achieve sub-500ms glass-to-glass latency for telepresence. A globally distributed client-server-client architecture ensures reliability and a seamless connection across worlds.
